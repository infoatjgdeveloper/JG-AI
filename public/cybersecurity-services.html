Enterprise Cybersecurity Services

Security at JGAI is not a feature bolted onto projects; it is a dedicated business. BRJU Infosec Inc., our North American cybersecurity entity, provides enterprise security services to clients worldwide, protecting the same production systems our engineering teams build. From penetration testing to fully managed security operations, we secure cloud platforms, AI systems, web applications and corporate infrastructure across 37+ countries.

Core services

  • Penetration testing. Manual, scenario-driven testing of web applications, APIs, mobile apps, cloud environments and internal networks, with prioritised remediation guidance rather than raw scanner output.
  • SOC as a service. A 24/7 security operations centre that monitors your logs, endpoints and cloud accounts, triages alerts and responds to incidents so your team does not have to build one in-house.
  • Zero trust architecture. Design and implementation of identity-first security: least-privilege access, network segmentation, device posture checks and continuous verification.
  • AI-powered threat detection. Machine learning models that surface anomalies and emerging attack patterns faster than signature-based tools.
  • Compliance and data protection. HIPAA-aligned controls for healthcare workloads, plus regional compliance delivery in Europe through IA7 Global Tech.

Securing AI systems

Because JGAI is also an AI research company, we secure what most firms cannot: the AI layer itself. That includes defending large language model applications against prompt injection and data exfiltration, protecting proprietary model weights, hardening agent frameworks that can take real actions, and building safe deployment pipelines for AI in regulated environments. If your company is adopting AI, this is the security expertise that generic providers lack.

How we engage

Frequently asked questions

How is a JGAI penetration test different from a vulnerability scan? A scan lists potential issues; our testers chain them the way real attackers do, demonstrate actual impact safely, and deliver a prioritised fix plan. Every finding includes reproduction steps and remediation guidance your engineers can act on immediately.

What does SOC as a service cost compared to building in-house? A 24/7 internal SOC requires at least five analysts plus tooling, typically a seven-figure annual cost. Our managed SOC delivers the same coverage as a monthly subscription scoped to your log volume and asset count.

Do you only secure systems that JGAI built? No. Most security engagements cover systems built by other vendors or in-house teams. We assess whatever you run today, cloud, on-premises or hybrid, and AI systems from any provider.
