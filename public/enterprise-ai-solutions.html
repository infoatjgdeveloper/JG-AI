What we build

Our enterprise AI practice covers the full lifecycle, from research and prototyping to deployment and 24/7 operation. Typical engagements include custom large language models fine-tuned on your private data, retrieval-augmented generation systems that connect models to your document stores and databases, autonomous AI agents that execute multi-step business workflows, computer vision systems for inspection and analytics, and forecasting models for demand, pricing and risk.

Every system is engineered for production from day one. That means measurable accuracy targets, evaluation suites, monitoring dashboards, rollback plans and clear cost controls, not just a demo notebook. We deploy on your cloud of choice, including AWS, Azure and Google Cloud, or on-premises where data residency requires it.

How an engagement works

  • Discovery. We map your use case, data and success metrics in a structured discovery call.
  • Solution design. Our architects propose the model strategy, infrastructure and integration plan with transparent pricing.
  • Agile build. Sprint-based development with demos every cycle, so you see progress weekly rather than at the end.
  • Deployment and support. Production launch with monitoring, retraining schedules and 24/7 global support.

Frequently asked questions

How long does an enterprise AI project take? Discovery to production typically runs 6 to 12 weeks depending on scope. Smaller systems such as internal copilots or RAG assistants often ship in under a month, while full data-platform builds run longer. You will have a working demo within the first sprint.

Do you work with our existing stack? Yes. We integrate with your current cloud accounts, databases, identity providers and APIs rather than forcing a migration. Deliverables include documented APIs and infrastructure-as-code so your team owns everything we build.

Who owns the models and the data? You do. Models fine-tuned on your data, the training pipelines and all project IP transfer to you under the engagement contract. Your data is never used to train systems for other clients.
